== Description ==
Eva Blog is a lightweight theme for blogs or photography portfolios. Its design is clean and minimalistic, focusing all the attention on the content. With a retro vibe, Eva is perfect for those pages that want to impress with stunning visuals and content. It is based on the Gutenberg block editor and its galleries.
